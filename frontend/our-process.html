OUR PROCESS

Top Apartment Locators in Houston, TX

Connect

We’ll pair you with one of our team members to go over exactly what you’re looking for in your new home.

Communication is key, and understanding your wants and needs is crucial to helping you find the right space for your lifestyle.

02

Expert Market Knowledge

Every apartment locator at Oakline Homes and Realty is a licensed Real Estate Agent.

They'll curate a precise list of apartments that match exactly what you’re looking for in your new home.

Their insights and expertise are an invaluable resource when navigating Houston’s rental market.

Narrow the Options

This is where you let your locator know what you like & what you don’t like.

You can let them know if you’re loving a certain community, or if you’re not really feeling the vibe.

We work with EVERY apartment community in Houston, so our agents are able to provide honest, objective, opinions & advice that will help you narrow your search down to your top picks.

03
04

Tour Time

It’s all led up to this! Once you’ve narrowed down your top choices your agent will confirm your availability to tour apartments in person or virtually.

They’ll make the calls and schedule the appointments, all you have to do is show up to the community with a government ID & face mask and you’re all set - it’s tour time!

Pro Tip: Make sure to take videos and pictures of the apartments you tour so can review them later!

Apply for the Perfect Home

The apartments pricing is perfect, the availability aligned precisely with your move in date, and the property tour was phenomenal. It’s time for you to submit your rental application and lock it in!

This is where we ask YOU for a small favor.

⭐ Kindly list "Oakline Homes and Realty" as your apartment locator referral on your application for your agent to get credit for your move-in.
05

Move in!

Once we’ve found you the perfect home and your application is approved, all that’s left to do is move in and enjoy your fancy new apartment!

Listing Oakline Homes and Realty as your referral allows us to help more renters just like you move into their new home.

Until next time, we'll be here if you need us!
