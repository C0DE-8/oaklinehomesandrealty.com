Application Fee

Each adult applicant (18+) is required to pay a non-refundable $75 application fee for credit, background, and rental history screening. The total cost depends on the number of adult applicants.

Reserve Your Unit (Optional Holding Fee)

If you select a property, you may choose to secure it with a holding fee while your application is being processed.

Holding Fee: typically $100-$500 depending on the unit.

  • Reserves the property during the application process.
  • May be applied toward move-in costs if approved.
  • Terms will be clearly confirmed before payment.
